Tomoko Kawase


Tomoko Kawase, or known as Tommy is a Japanese musician, originating from Kyoto. She was born on February 6, 1975, which means she's around 49 years old right now. She started off as the main vocalist for the band "The Brilliant Green." She would then later start her debut as her two alter egos: "Tommy february6", and "Tommy heavenly6".


She would start her career in 1995 after being asked to join as a lead vocalist in rock band "The Brilliant Green" after performing at an amateur singing contest. She would release around 17 singles and 8 albums. (My favorite album is "Los Angeles"!) In 2001, she would debut as "Tommy february6", a cutesy persona who would act much differently compared to the "innocent" surface. The style was super different from The Brilliant Green though, as her music as Tommy february6 would be British-American 80's Synthpop inspired. She would release 8 EPs, 2 mini albums and 5 albums. (My favorite EP is "Lonely in Gorgeous", which was also used as the opening for the anime "Paradise Kiss"!) Then in 2003, Tommy would debut as another persona, "Tommy heavenly6", the supressed feelings of Tommy february6 that was derived from a dream. She would release 2 EPs, 9 singles, 2 mini albums, and 6 albums. (My favorite album is her debut album, "Tommy heavenly6"!)
